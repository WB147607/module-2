Torta

Ingredients Directions
4 rolls (bolillos, teleras or French rolls) 1.In a gallon size resealable bag, combine lime juice, crushed garlic, orange juice, cilantro, salt, pepper, olive oil, jalapeno, and vinegar. Squeeze it around to mix it up.
1 1/4lb pork leg with a little fat. The fat keeps the meat moist and adds flavor 2.Put the entire flank steak into the resealable bag. Seal it up tight. Make sure all the meat is exposed to the marinade, squishing the bag around to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
1 1/2 cups refried beans 3.Heat an outdoor grill to high heat
1 cup mayonnaise 4.Remove the flank steak from the marinade, and discard excess marinade. Cook on the grill for 7 to 10 minutes per side.
2 small white onions 5.Once done, remove from heat and let rest 10 minutes.
3 or 4 small plum tomatoes 6.Slice the roll in half and generously spread mayonnaise on both pieces.
2 small avocados 7.Over medium heat, toast the bread with mayonnaise in the same pan that you browned the pork
7 oz. can of jalapeño chiles in vinegar 8.spread refried beans on the bottom half of the bread.Then, top with the browned pork
1/2 tsp sea salt 9.Top the pork with shredded lettuce, sliced tomato, onion, and vinegar jalapeños.On the top half of the bread add avocado slices.

Sopa de Fideos

Ingredients Directions
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes 1.Place the tomatoes, onion, and garlic into a blender, and pulse several times to get the mixture moving; blend until smooth, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
1 small onion, chopped 2.Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat, and stir in the pasta. Fry the pasta gently, stirring often, until the pasta is golden brown, 2 to 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
1 large clove garlic, coarsely chopped 4.Pour the tomato mixture into a large saucepan, and stir in chicken broth and noodles. Bring to a boil, and reduce heat to medium low; simmer until the noodles are tender, 7 to 8 minutes. Season to taste with salt and black pepper, and stir in the cilantro; simmer 2 more minutes to cook cilantro.
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 (7 ounce) package fideo pasta, uncooked
3 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
freshly ground black pepper and coarse salt to taste
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
